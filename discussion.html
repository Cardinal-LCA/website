Cardinal LCA founders present at the ACLCA 2021
Oct 10, 2021

The American Center for Life Cycle Assessment (ACLCA) 2021 Conference was held virtually from Sept 21-24, wherein our team presented the foundational work behind Cardinal LCA, done as part of the seminar at Harvard GSD instructed by Prof. Jonathan Grinham. Project titled ‘Early Stage Design LCA Calculation Framework within Grasshopper’ was presented as part of the Buildings & Power category.The conference proceedings shall be published soon on the ACLCA 2021 webpage.

It’s a win! Harvard WECode Startup Pitch Competition 2021 - 3rd Place
April 25, 2021

The co-founders won the 3rd prize at the Harvard WECode Startup Pitch Competition 2021, where the six finalist teams each gave an 8 min pitch followed by a 3 minute Q&A with the judges. Read more about the event here. The Harvard WECode (Women Engineers Code) Conference is organized by undergraduate women at Harvard University is the largest student-run women in tech conference in the world.
