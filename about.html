- About us -

What do we do?

Motivation

We are a team driven to reduce the environmental impact caused by the Architecture, Engineering & Construction industries. Embodied carbon within the industry is clandestinely contributing higher and higher to the overall Global Warming Potential. Therefore, we are motivated to tackle this issue.

Goal

We start with early-stage environmental impact analysis. But we don’t stop there - our goal is to bring all the partakers in a building design, analysis, construction and demolition process on board the Cardinal LCA platform in the future for effective LCA assessment.
